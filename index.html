Meet our doctors

Two passionate dentists united by a shared commitment to exceptional, patient-first care.

Dr. Emily Foldes

DMD · Boston University · Class of 2017

Dr. Emily Foldes

Doctor of Dental Medicine · General Dentist

Dr. Emily Foldes graduated from Boston University's Goldman School of Dental Medicine with her Doctor of Dental Medicine in 2017. After earning her DMD, she went on to complete further training in the Advanced Education in General Dentistry residency at the University of Connecticut.

Prior to dental training, Dr. Emily graduated from The University of Western Ontario with an Honours Specialization in Biology.

As a dental student, she was involved in the school's social committee and served as Social Chair and President of Alpha Omega Dental Fraternity.

Dr. Emily prides herself on delivering high quality dental care and especially loves building relationships with her patients. Dr. Emily can also provide therapeutic injectables like Botox.

Outside of the office, Dr. Emily likes to stay active, take group fitness classes, explore new restaurants, and travel!

✨ Fun Fact

In 2021, Dr. Emily published her first children's book — The Tooth Troopers!

Dr. Matt Librach

DMD · University of Pennsylvania

Dr. Matt Librach

Doctor of Dental Medicine · General Dentist

Dr. Matt Librach graduated from The University of Pennsylvania School of Dental Medicine (UPenn) with his Doctor of Dental Medicine degree. While at UPenn, Dr. Matt completed an honorary degree in clinical dentistry, allowing him to specialize in more advanced restorative procedures.

Prior to dental training, Dr. Matt graduated from The University of Western Ontario (UWO) with an Honours Specialization in Psychology.

While in dental school, Dr. Matt co-founded the Society of Oral Oncology and served as Social Chair of the Alpha Omega Dental Fraternity at the University of Pennsylvania.

Dr. Matt strives to provide the most up-to-date and technologically advanced dental treatment. Together with his team at Wilson Family Dental, he looks forward to providing the highest quality of dental care.

During his free time, Dr. Matt loves to participate in all sporting activities — especially golfing in the summer and skiing in the winter.

✨ Fun Fact

Dr. Matt was a varsity Fencer at UWO and was awarded as an All Canadian Fencer.
